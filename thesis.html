University Students' Travel Patterns 大学生出行模式研究

This study on the travel patterns of university students in North Carolina was my master’s project. I analyzed the survey data collected from 6 universities and summarized students’ travel behaviors. I also calculated factors that can be applied to the development of trip generation, trip distribution, time of day distribution, directional split, and modal split parts of the travel demand model for university students’ trips. The thesis received a High-Pass (highest grade) for evaluation.
